Get Started with Teams

Its really easy to start using ScoreOnline to manage your teams. Once you begin managing teams you can truly utilise ScoreOnline.

Adding a Team

If you're logged in as a club its really simple to create a team. Head over to the teams tab, and then click the plus button. You'll need to fill out a form which grabs a few details about the team.

Allowing external team signup

Wouldn't it be nice if you could allow teams to automatically sign up into your club. Well its super easy to enable this. Click the large sharing tools box on the home screen of ScoreOnline then select Team direct sign up. You'll be presented with a few bits and peices. The first is an embed code, you can place this on your website and allow teams to sign up from your website. The second is a direct link. If you give someone this link they'll be able to sign up as a team into your club. Pretty cool eh!

Thanks for using ScoreOnline. I hope you found it easy to create teams, see you next time.
